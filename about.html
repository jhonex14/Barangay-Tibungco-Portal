About Barangay Tibungco

A progressive, safe, and digitally-empowered community dedicated to providing efficient and transparent public service.

To deliver fast, transparent, and accessible barangay services to all residents through modern technology, while maintaining peace, order, and sustainable community development.

Our Vision

Barangay Tibungco envisions a highly developed, safe, and environmentally sustainable community with empowered citizens actively participating in local governance.
